About Me

I’m a tattoo artist with over a decade of experience, focusing on clean execution, strong composition and healed longevity. My practice blends precise linework with soft, controlled shading to create pieces that age gracefully on the skin.

Whether it’s a delicate fine-line micro piece or a bold black & grey composition, I work closely with clients to refine ideas into intentional designs. From placement and scale to flow with anatomy, every decision is guided by how the tattoo will heal and live.
